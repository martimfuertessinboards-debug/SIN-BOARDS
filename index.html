The Story

Born from a lack of what should already exist.

SIN Boards started in Portugal from a simple frustration: it was far harder than it should be to find high-quality skimboards with real identity and serious intent.

What began as a personal obsession with skimboarding became the beginning of a bigger idea — to build something sharper, cleaner and more meaningful from the ground up.

SIN Boards is not being built as a generic surf-style brand. It is being built with the intention to create something more focused, more refined and more memorable.

What is SIN Boards

A skimboard brand project based in Portugal.

SIN Boards is a developing skimboard brand created in Portugal with the goal of building high-quality performance skimboards for riders who want more control, progression and identity.

The project is currently in development and focused on creating a stronger local presence for skimboarding while building a serious foundation for future board production.

Philosophy

Identity before noise.

The idea behind SIN Boards is not to chase trends or imitate what already exists. It is to build a skimboard project with stronger presence, cleaner design and a more intentional direction.

Performance matters. Progression matters. But so does identity — the feeling that what you ride actually stands for something.

This brand is being shaped around clarity, restraint and a deeper sense of purpose rather than empty hype.

In Development

This is the first stage of the project.

SIN Boards is currently in development. The brand identity, product direction and first concepts are all being refined with care and long-term intention.

At this stage, the focus is on building the right foundation: understanding materials, refining concepts, exploring manufacturing and shaping a brand that feels real from day one.

This is not a finished product launch. It is the beginning of a serious process.

“Built slowly. Built properly. Built with meaning.”
